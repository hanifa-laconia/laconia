WELCOME TO LACONIA GROUP LIMITED

A leader in commodity trading and related businesses, Laconia Group Limited boasts over 20 years of global experience. Since its establishment in 1998, the group has been dedicated to providing tailor-made solutions that meet the specific needs of its customers. Through its diverse portfolio, Laconia Group offers expertise in construction solutions and design projects, significantly contributing to regional and international markets.

With a strong presence in West Africa, Laconia Group collaborates on various impactful projects, extending its reach and influence. The group's specialization in advanced fire prevention systems utilizes state-of-the-art technology developed in partnership with leading manufacturers, ensuring top-tier safety solutions.

In addition, Laconia Group is engaged in the supply and export of metal products, maintaining strong relationships with major industry players. The group also deals in the general trading of building materials, metals, and agricultural products, adopting a comprehensive approach to meet the varied demands of its clientele.
