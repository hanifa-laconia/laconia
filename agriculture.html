MINERAL FERTILIZERS

LACONIA GROUP LIMITED is an international supplier of mineral fertilizers for agriculture. The key area of LACONIA GROUP LIMITED activity is the supply of nitrogen, potash and complex fertilizers, feed additives, as well as chlorine-free premium fertilizers with micronutrients.

Combined capacity of the partners manufacturing plants makes LACONIA GROUP LIMITED a leading supplier of mineral fertilizers to many countries. We also offer complex business solutions based on our vast experience in agriculture.

LACONIA GROUP LIMITED offers a vast range of fertilizers for different crops: from cereals and vegetables to fruit and berries. Granular fertilizers for open ground, water-soluble fertilizers used in professional greenhouses, in fertigation and for foliar application.

If you are looking for a reliable supplier of high-quality fertilizers, LACONIA GROUP LIMITED is a perfect choice. Reliability, quality and environmental safety are the main advantages that we offer our customers.
