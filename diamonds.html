Exquisite Diamonds

Laconia Group is a premier global supplier of rough and polished auction-grade diamonds. We specialize in exceptional diamonds of 10+ carats and rare fancy colored diamonds.

A Legacy of Brilliance

Laconia Group Limited stands as a pillar in the global diamond industry, serving as one of the largest suppliers of both rough and polished diamonds to the world market. Our operations encompass the entire value chain—from strategic mining interests and ethical sourcing to world-class cutting and polishing.

We provide a curated selection of auction-grade diamonds that meet the highest standards of the four Cs. Our commitment to transparency and excellence ensures that every diamond in our inventory is a masterpiece of nature, refined by human mastery.

Certified Excellence

Every diamond in our portfolio undergoes rigorous analysis and certification by the world's leading gemological laboratories, including GIA, IGI, and HRD. We provide full provenance and technical characteristics for each diamond, ensuring absolute trust and investment security.
