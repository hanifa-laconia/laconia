WATER MIST SYSTEMS

Water Mist Systems are a cutting-edge development in fire suppression technology. Their modern design offers exceptional efficiency, using less water while providing superior fire control and minimizing potential water damage.

Water Mist Systems use fine water droplets to extinguish fires, providing precise and efficient fire control compared to traditional sprinklers. They activate via heat, smoke, or manual triggers and are installed in areas like hotels, malls, industrial and commercial buildings, and in many other critical areas.
